ZZ (tr@zz.info): What are the main things to think about for a college ranking high in organic search?
Chatbot response: 
The main things to think about for a college ranking high in organic search are optimizing their website for sitelinks, optimizing images for search results, optimizing their Google Business Profile, and optimizing content for questions and answers. Optimizing for sitelinks will give users the opportunity to click through to different areas of the website, increasing CTR and improving user experience. Image optimization involves including keywords in the file name, title text, and alt text, as well as compressing images to decrease load time. Optimizing the Google Business Profile will increase visibility in local search results and increase inclusion in special SERP features. Finally, optimizing content for questions and answers will increase the chances of appearing in Google’s “People Also Ask” feature or rich snippet position.
